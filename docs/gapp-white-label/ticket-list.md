# Ticket List

## Main Tickets View

![Tickets view](/img/gapp-white-label/ticket-list.jpg)

This is the ticket window in Gapp.

1. On the top left is the **burger menu icon**.
2. On the top right is the **filter menu**.
3. Now, on each card, we have on top the **ticket title**.
4. In the bottom left of each ticket, we have a small icon telling us the **ticket priority**.
5. In the top left of each ticket, we have a **ticket status** indicator. Just like GLPI:
   - New
   - Processing (Assigned)
   - Processing (Planned)
   - Waiting
   - Solved
   - Closed
6. In the top right of each ticket, we have an indicator of the **ticket type**:
   - Incident
   - Request
7. Also in the top right of each ticket, next to the ticket type, we have the **ticket number**.
8. Just below the ticket title appears the ticket's **last update**.
9. Finally, there is a **New ticket button**.

*Note: All tickets are sorted using the last update from newer to older.*

## Filters Menu

![Filters menu](/img/gapp-white-label/filters.jpg)

With access to the **Filter Menu**, the following screen is shown.

1. Your **opened** tickets (My tickets).
2. **You can reach all tickets** based on your permissions (All tickets).
3. Your **solved tickets** pending to close (Tickets to close).
4. The **content filter** allows you to make a text-based fast search on the ticket title or description.
5. **Sort tickets** by last update.
6. **Sort tickets** by creation date.
7. **Sort tickets** by priority.
8. **Sort tickets** by the SLA due date.
9. Show **tickets created from a date**.
10. This button allows you to use **custom searches** for tickets, which must be previously configured (tickets from a group or in a specific state, for example).
11. From here you can also directly **access a ticket by its ID**.
